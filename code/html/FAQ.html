What types of coffee do you offer ?

We offer espresso, cappuccino, latte, americano, macchiato, and mocha, with various milk alternatives and customization options.

Yes, you can track your order after purchase through a link provided via email or by logging into your account on our website.

Are the coffee machines under warranty ?

Yes, all our coffee machines come with a warranty. The duration and terms of the warranty vary by model, and details are provided with each purchase.

Do you sell organic or fair trade coffee ?

Yes, we offer both organic and fair trade coffee options, ensuring sustainable and ethically sourced products.
